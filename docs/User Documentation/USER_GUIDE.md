## Getting Started

### What You'll Need

- Email address for account registration
- Academic information (GPA, subjects, grades)
- Clear idea of your interests and career goals
- Budget range for tuition (optional but recommended)

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit SIRA Platform**
   - Navigate to the SIRA homepage
   - Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right

2. **Choose Sign-Up Method**
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Other social providers

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add basic information
   - Set your preferences
   - Upload profile picture (optional)

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see three main sections:

**Profiles**
- Create and manage academic profiles
- Each profile represents a different academic path
- Example: "Computer Science Track", "Business Studies", "Medicine Path"

**Recommendations**
- View AI-generated program suggestions
- See match scores and reasoning
- Compare programs side-by-side
- Access recommendation history

**Chat**
- Ask questions to the AI advisor
- Get instant guidance
- Clarify recommendations
- Explore career options

## Creating Your First Profile

Your profile is the foundation for personalized recommendations. The more detailed and honest you are, the better your results!

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Click **"Create New Profile"** from your dashboard
2. Give your profile a meaningful name
   - Good: "Engineering Track 2026"
   - Avoid: "Profile 1"

3. Select profile status:
   - **Draft**: Still working on it
   - **Active**: Ready for recommendations
   - **Archived**: No longer using

### Step 2: Academic Record

#### Current Status
Choose what best describes you:
- **High School Student**: Currently in secondary education
- **University Student**: Enrolled in higher education
- **Graduate**: Completed your degree
- **Career Switcher**: Looking to change fields

#### Institution Details
```
Current Institution: Lincoln High School
Field of Study: Science and Technology
Language Preference: English (or French/Arabic)
```

#### Academic Performance

**GPA Entry (0-20 Scale)**
- Enter your cumulative GPA
- Example: 15.5/20

**Subject Grades**
Add individual subject performance:
```
Subject: Mathematics
Grade: 18.5/20
Weight/Coefficient: 4.0

Subject: Physics  
Grade: 17.0/20
Weight/Coefficient: 3.0

Subject: Computer Science
Grade: 19.0/20
Weight/Coefficient: 2.0
```

**Tips:**
- Include your strongest subjects (these influence recommendations)
- Be honest about grades (AI needs accurate data)
- Add weight/coefficients if your school uses them

#### Transcript Upload (Optional)
- Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
- Maximum size: 5MB
- Keeps your grades organized and verifiable

### Step 3: Interests & Skills

Help the AI understand what motivates you:

#### Favorite Subjects
List subjects you genuinely enjoy:
```
Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics
Economics, Business, Marketing
Biology, Chemistry, Medicine
```

#### Subjects to Avoid
Be honest about what you don't enjoy:
```
History, Literature
Art, Music Theory
```

This helps filter out programs you won't like!

#### Soft Skills
What are you good at beyond academics?
```
- Problem Solving
- Leadership
- Communication
- Teamwork
- Critical Thinking
- Creativity
```

#### Hobbies & Interests
```
- Programming & Coding
- Reading & Research
- Sports & Athletics
- Volunteering
- Music Production
```

These provide context for career fit!

### Step 4: Preferences & Constraints

#### Geographic Preference
Where do you want to study?
```
- Morocco (specific city: Casablanca, Rabat, etc.)
- France
- International (specify region)
- Flexible / Open to anywhere
```

#### Budget Range
Be realistic about what you can afford:
```
Minimum: 50,000 MAD/year
Maximum: 150,000 MAD/year
```

**Note:** Include living costs, not just tuition!

#### Career Goals
Describe your aspirations:
```
"I want to become a software engineer specializing in 
AI and machine learning. I'm interested in working for 
tech companies or startups. Long-term, I'd like to lead 
engineering teams."
```

**Be Specific:**
- "Data scientist in healthcare"
- "Investment banker in renewable energy"
- "Something with computers"
- "I don't know"

### Step 5: Review & Save

1. **Review Your Information**
   - Check for typos
   - Verify GPA calculation
   - Ensure budget is realistic

2. **Save as Draft** or **Activate Profile**
   - Draft: Continue editing later
   - Active: Ready for recommendations

3. **Auto-Save Feature**
   - Your progress is automatically saved
   - Resume anytime without losing data

## Getting AI Recommendations

### Generating Your First Recommendation

Recommendations are now fully integrated into the chat interface for a more interactive and personalized experience.

1. **Start a Chat Session**
   - From dashboard, click "Chat"
   - A profile is automatically attached to your session

2. **Click "Generate Recommendations" Button**
   - Look for the "Generate Recommendations" button in the chat interface
   - AI analyzes your profile and chat history
   - Searches program database with context from your conversation
   - Generates personalized suggestions

3. **Watch the Magic Happen**
   - Real-time streaming response directly in chat
   - See the AI's thinking process
   - Takes 10-30 seconds

4. **Discuss Your Recommendations**
   - Ask follow-up questions about specific programs
   - Compare options in the conversation
   - Get clarification on requirements
   - AI remembers context from the entire session

### What You'll Receive

**Recommendation Structure:**

```
Top Matches (3-5 programs)

1. Computer Science - UM6P (Match: 95%)
   Excellent fit for your profile
   Strong AI/ML focus
   Within budget: 120,000 MAD/year
   Location: Benguerir, Morocco
   
   Why This Match:
   - Your math and CS grades (18+/20) exceed requirements
   - Career goal aligns with program outcomes
   - Research opportunities in AI
   
   Requirements:
   - Minimum GPA: 16/20
   - Entrance exam required
   - English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or equivalent)

2. Software Engineering - Al Akhawayn (Match: 89%)
   [Similar structure...]

3. Data Science - ENSIAS (Match: 85%)
   [Similar structure...]
```

### Understanding Match Scores

**Match Score Breakdown:**

- **90-100%** - Excellent Match
  - You exceed all requirements
  - Perfect alignment with goals
  - High acceptance probability

- **75-89%** - Good Match
  - Meet most requirements
  - Good fit with minor gaps
  - Strong candidate

- **60-74%** - Moderate Match
  - Meet minimum requirements
  - Some misalignment
  - Consider as backup

- **Below 60%** - Stretch Goal
  - Below requirements
  - Significant effort needed
  - Apply with caution

## Using the Chat Interface

### Starting a Conversation

1. **Click "Chat" Tab**
2. **Profile Automatically Attached**
   - Your profile is linked to the chat session
   - AI has full context of your academic background
   - Recommendations are optimized based on chat history
3. **Type Your Question or Generate Recommendations**
4. **Get Instant AI Response**

### Generating Recommendations in Chat

The chat interface now includes a **"Generate Recommendations" button** that:
- Analyzes your profile AND your conversation history
- Provides context-aware program suggestions
- Allows immediate discussion of results
- Enables refinement through follow-up questions

**Why Chat-Based Recommendations Are Better:**
- AI understands your preferences from the conversation
- You can immediately ask questions about recommended programs
- Recommendations improve as you provide more context
- Seamless experience without switching pages

### Effective Questions

**Good Questions:**

```
"What's the difference between Computer Science and 
Software Engineering programs?"

"Which program has better job placement rates?"

"Can I switch from Engineering to Business after 
first year at UM6P?"

"What scholarships are available for international 
students?"

"Is my GPA of 14/20 competitive for ENSIAS?"
```

**Less Effective:**

```
"What should I study?" (too vague)
"Tell me everything about universities" (too broad)
"Which is better?" (need context)
```

### Chat Features

- **Real-time Streaming**: See responses as they're generated
- **Context-Aware**: AI remembers your profile and previous questions
- **Follow-ups**: Ask clarifying questions
- **Save Conversations**: Return to past chats anytime

## Managing Multiple Profiles

### Why Create Multiple Profiles?

**Common Use Cases:**

1. **Different Academic Paths**
   ```
   Profile 1: "Engineering Track"
   Profile 2: "Business Studies"
   Profile 3: "Medicine Path"
   ```

2. **Different Scenarios**
   ```
   Profile 1: "Best Case" (if I improve GPA to 18/20)
   Profile 2: "Current" (GPA 15.5/20)
   Profile 3: "Backup Options" (minimum requirements)
   ```

3. **Different Locations**
   ```
   Profile 1: "Morocco Programs"
   Profile 2: "France Programs"
   Profile 3: "International"
   ```

### Profile Management

**View All Profiles**
- Grid or list view
- Status badges (Draft, Active, Archived)
- Quick actions (Edit, Delete, Clone)

**Edit Profile**
- Update any information
- Changes don't affect past recommendations
- See improvement impact instantly

**Clone Profile**
- Duplicate existing profile
- Modify specific details
- Compare different scenarios

**Archive Profile**
- Hide from active list
- Keep recommendation history
- Can reactivate anytime

**Delete Profile**
- Permanently remove
- Also deletes all recommendations
- Cannot be undone

## Tips for Best Results

### Profile Creation Tips

1. **Be Honest**
   - Accurate grades lead to realistic suggestions
   - Don't inflate abilities
   - The AI can't help if the data is wrong

2. **Be Specific**
   - Vague goals = vague recommendations
   - Detail your interests
   - Explain your motivation

3. **Include Context**
   - Family background
   - Financial situation
   - Location constraints
   - Health considerations

4. **Update Regularly**
   - New grades? Update profile!
   - Changed interests? Reflect that!
   - Fresh recommendations use latest data

### Getting Better Recommendations

1. **Complete All Fields**
   - More data = better matching
   - Optional fields matter
   - Upload transcripts if possible

2. **Be Realistic About Constraints**
   - Budget: Include all costs
   - Location: Consider travel
   - Time: Can you study full-time?

3. **Use Multiple Profiles**
   - Test different scenarios
   - Compare outcomes
   - Make informed decisions

4. **Provide Feedback**
   - Rate recommendations
   - Explain what was helpful/unhelpful
   - Helps improve the AI

### Chat Best Practices

1. **Ask Specific Questions**
   - Instead of: "What should I study?"
   - Ask: "Is Computer Science or Data Science better for AI careers?"

2. **Provide Context**
   - Mention your profile
   - Reference previous recommendations
   - Explain your situation

3. **Follow Up**
   - Ask clarifying questions
   - Request more details
   - Explore alternatives

4. **Save Important Conversations**
   - Bookmark useful exchanges
   - Export for reference
   - Share with advisors

## FAQ

### Account & Access

**Q: Is SIRA free to use?**
A: Yes, basic features are free. Premium features may be available in the future.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### Profiles & Recommendations

**Q: How many profiles can I create?**
A: Unlimited! Create as many as you need to explore different paths.

**Q: How often should I generate new recommendations?**
A: Generate new recommendations when:
- Your grades change significantly
- Your interests evolve
- New programs are added to the database
- You want fresh perspectives

**Q: Why don't my recommendations match my expectations?**
A: Common reasons:
- Your GPA may not meet minimum requirements
- Budget constraints limiting options
- Geographic preferences too narrow
- Career goals misaligned with subjects

**Q: Can I save recommendations for later?**
A: Yes, all recommendations are automatically saved in your history.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The AI is taking too long to respond**
A: Normal generation takes 10-30 seconds. If it exceeds 60 seconds:
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few minutes

**Q: I can't see the streaming response**
A: Ensure:
- Your browser is up to date
- JavaScript is enabled
- Ad blockers aren't interfering
- You're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

**Q: My uploaded transcript isn't showing**
A: Verify:
- File format is PDF, JPG, or PNG
- File size is under 5MB
- File isn't corrupted
- Try re-uploading

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use:
- Industry-standard encryption
- Secure authentication (Clerk)
- Regular security audits
- GDPR-compliant practices

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: Only you. Your data is private and never shared without consent.

**Q: Does SIRA sell my data?**
A: No. We never sell user data to third parties.

### Recommendations Quality

**Q: Are these official admission guarantees?**
A: No. SIRA provides guidance based on data, but:
- Universities make final decisions
- Requirements can change
- Each application is unique
- Always verify with universities

**Q: How up-to-date is the program database?**
A: We update regularly, but always confirm:
- Tuition fees with universities
- Admission requirements
- Program availability
- Deadlines

**Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?**
A: That's okay! The AI provides suggestions based on data patterns. You know yourself best. Use recommendations as one input in your decision-making process.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" or "Account not found"

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check caps lock is off
3. Try password reset
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser

#### Profile Won't Save

**Problem**: Changes disappear or error message appears

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify all required fields are filled
3. Ensure GPA is in valid range (0-20)
4. Check budget min < max
5. Try saving as draft first

#### Recommendations Not Loading

**Problem**: Stuck at "Generating..." or error message

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh page
2. Check if profile is active (not draft)
3. Verify all required profile fields completed
4. Try different profile
5. Contact support if persists

#### Chat Not Responding

**Problem**: Messages sent but no AI response

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page
3. Try shorter, more specific questions
4. Verify you're logged in
5. Clear browser cache

## Glossary

**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**
AI technique combining information retrieval with text generation for accurate recommendations.

**Match Score**
Percentage indicating how well a program aligns with your profile (0-100%).

**Profile Status**
- **Draft**: In progress, not ready
- **Active**: Complete, ready for recommendations
- **Archived**: No longer in use, hidden from view

**SSE (Server-Sent Events)**
Technology enabling real-time streaming of AI responses.

**Vector Database**
Specialized database storing program information for semantic search.

**Semantic Search**
AI-powered search understanding meaning, not just keywords.
