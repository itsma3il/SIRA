## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up / Sign In

1. Visit the SIRA platform
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Sign In"**
3. Create an account using:
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Or other supported providers

### 2. Dashboard Overview

After signing in, you'll see your dashboard with three main sections:

- ** Profiles**: Create and manage academic profiles
- ** Recommendations**: View AI-generated program suggestions
- ** Chat**: Interact with the AI advisor

## Creating Your Profile

Your profile is the foundation for personalized recommendations. The more detailed you are, the better the suggestions!

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Click **"New Profile"** from the dashboard
2. Give your profile a name (e.g., "Computer Science Track")
3. Choose a status:
   - **Draft**: Work in progress
   - **Active**: Ready for recommendations
   - **Archived**: No longer using

### Step 2: Academic Record

Fill in your academic information:

**Current Status**
- High School Student
- University Student
- Graduate
- Professional

**Institution Details**
- Current school/university name
- Field of study
- Language preference

**Academic Performance**
- GPA (0-20 scale)
- Individual subject grades
- Transcript upload (optional)

**Example:**
```
Current Status: High School Student
Institution: Lincoln High School
Field: Science and Technology
GPA: 15.5/20
Language: English

Subject Grades:
- Mathematics: 18.5/20 (Weight: 4.0)
- Physics: 17.0/20 (Weight: 3.0)
- Computer Science: 19.0/20 (Weight: 2.0)
```

### Step 3: Preferences & Interests

Help the AI understand what you're looking for:

**Favorite Subjects**
- List subjects you excel at and enjoy
- Examples: Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics

**Skills & Hobbies**
- Soft skills: Problem Solving, Leadership, Teamwork
- Hobbies: Coding, Robotics, Writing

**Geographic Preferences**
- Preferred regions: Europe, North America, Asia, etc.
- Specific countries (optional)

**Budget Range**
- Minimum annual budget: €5,000
- Maximum annual budget: €15,000

**Career Goals**
- Describe your dream career
- Example: "Software Engineer at a leading tech company"

### Step 4: Save and Activate

1. Review your information
2. Click **"Save Draft"** to save progress
3. Click **"Activate Profile"** when ready for recommendations

 **Tip**: You can create multiple profiles to explore different academic paths!

## Getting Recommendations

### Automatic Recommendations

Once your profile is active:

1. Go to the **Recommendations** tab
2. Click **"Generate Recommendations"**
3. Wait 10-30 seconds for AI processing
4. View your personalized results

### Manual Search

Use the search feature to find specific programs:

1. Enter your query: *"Computer Science programs in Switzerland"*
2. Select your profile
3. Click **"Search"**

### Understanding Results

Each recommendation includes:

**University & Program**
- University name and ranking
- Program name and degree type
- Location and language

**Match Score** (0-100%)
- How well you fit the program
- Based on your academic profile and preferences

**Difficulty Level**
- High: Very competitive
- Medium: Moderately competitive
- Low: Accessible

**Reasoning**
- Why this program matches your profile
- Key factors considered

**Admission Requirements**
- Required GPA
- Language tests (TOEFL, IELTS)
- Additional requirements

**Costs & Financial Aid**
- Tuition fees
- Living costs estimate
- Scholarship opportunities

## Using the Chat Interface

The conversational AI advisor can answer questions and provide guidance.

### Starting a Chat

1. Click **"Chat"** from the dashboard
2. Click **"New Conversation"**
3. Select a profile (or "General" for profile-independent questions)
4. Give your conversation a title

### Example Questions

**Academic Programs:**
- "What are the best Computer Science programs in Europe?"
- "Which universities offer AI/ML specializations?"
- "Tell me about ETH Zurich's admission requirements"

**Career Guidance:**
- "What career paths are available for Computer Science graduates?"
- "Which skills should I develop for a career in Data Science?"

**Application Process:**
- "How do I prepare for university applications?"
- "What documents do I need for Swiss universities?"
- "When are application deadlines for Fall 2026?"

**Comparison:**
- "Compare MIT and Stanford for Computer Science"
- "ETH Zurich vs EPFL for Engineering"

### Chat Features

- **Real-time streaming**: See responses as they're generated
- **Context-aware**: The AI remembers your conversation history
- **Profile-based**: Answers tailored to your academic profile
- **Source references**: Citations from the knowledge base

### Managing Conversations

- **Continue**: Resume previous conversations
- **Rename**: Update conversation title
- **Delete**: Remove conversations you no longer need

## Understanding Recommendations

### Match Score Calculation

The AI considers:

1. **Academic Fit** (40%)
   - GPA match
   - Subject performance
   - Academic rigor

2. **Interest Alignment** (30%)
   - Favorite subjects
   - Career goals
   - Research interests

3. **Practical Constraints** (20%)
   - Budget compatibility
   - Geographic preference
   - Language requirements

4. **Success Probability** (10%)
   - Admission competitiveness
   - Historical acceptance data

### Difficulty Levels

**High Difficulty (90%+ match score)**
- Top-tier universities
- Highly competitive
- Strong academic record required
- Example: MIT, Stanford, ETH Zurich

**Medium Difficulty (70-89% match score)**
- Well-ranked universities
- Selective but accessible
- Good academic record sufficient
- Example: TU Munich, KU Leuven

**Low Difficulty (Below 70% match score)**
- Accessible programs
- Less competitive admission
- May have lower requirements

### Interpreting Reasoning

The AI explains its recommendations based on:
- **Strengths**: Your academic excellence areas
- **Alignment**: How your interests match the program
- **Opportunities**: Unique features of the program
- **Challenges**: Potential difficulties to prepare for

## Tips for Best Results

### Profile Accuracy

 **Do:**
- Be honest about your academic performance
- Include all relevant subjects
- Update your profile regularly
- Add detailed career goals

 **Don't:**
- Inflate grades or achievements
- Leave sections blank
- Use vague descriptions
- Create duplicate profiles without purpose

### Asking Good Questions

**Good Questions:**
- Specific: "What are admission requirements for ETH Zurich Computer Science?"
- Actionable: "How can I improve my chances for competitive programs?"
- Contextual: "Based on my profile, what are my backup options?"

**Vague Questions:**
- "Tell me about universities"
- "What should I study?"
- "Where should I go?"

### Using Feedback

After viewing recommendations:
-  Rate them (1-5 stars)
-  Leave comments
-  This improves future suggestions

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is SIRA free to use?**
A: Yes, SIRA is currently free during the beta period.

**Q: How often are recommendations updated?**
A: Recommendations are generated in real-time based on your latest profile data.

**Q: Can I use SIRA from any country?**
A: Yes, SIRA supports international users. However, the knowledge base focuses on European and North American universities.

### Profile & Recommendations

**Q: How many profiles can I create?**
A: You can create unlimited profiles to explore different academic paths.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
A: Recommendations are based on extensive academic data and AI analysis, but they should be verified with official university sources.

**Q: Can I get recommendations for graduate programs?**
A: Currently, SIRA focuses on undergraduate programs. Graduate program support is coming soon.

**Q: Why don't I see any recommendations?**
A: Ensure your profile is complete and set to "Active" status. Try generating recommendations manually.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: Only you can see your profiles. They are not shared with universities or other users.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data from your account settings.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The chat is not responding**
A: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or contact support.

**Q: I can't upload my transcript**
A: Ensure your file is:
- PDF, JPG, or PNG format
- Under 5MB in size
- Not corrupted

**Q: Recommendations are taking too long**
A: Initial generation can take 30-60 seconds. If it exceeds 2 minutes, try again or contact support.
